About Me

My name is Justin. I was born and raised in Missouri and moved to San Francisco upon graduating from the University of Missouri where I studied mechanical engineering. During that time I did some cool things, such as spend a year of high school in Colombia and conducted research to help advance our knowledge of nuclear fusion.

Now I focus the analytical skills and ruthless curiosity, instilled in me by my mentors, in the field software development. As an avid learner, I jump at the opportunity to dive into a new stack where I can engineer products that are elegant without being flashy and that are complex while appearing simple.
